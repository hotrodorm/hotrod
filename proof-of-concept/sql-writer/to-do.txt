  NonWindowableAggregationFunction<T>  WindowableAggregationFunction<T>.....:  :.....AnalyticFunction<T>
   ^                                    ^    ^                                          ^             ^

   |            NumericAggregationFunction  StringAggregationFunction  PositionalAnalyticFunction<?>  |
